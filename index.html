The new ADC

Xayah is a very different kind of adc, she does not rely on her auto attacks as heavily as other adc's, her ability to burst other adc's down can be very useful against a team of squishy champions. However, that doesn't effect her ability to kill Tanks. With armor penetration, even tanks are not a match for her. It is very important to be careful though, because her range is the shortest range of an adc, her positioning is essential. Her attack speed is very low as well. She benefits a lot from critical chance and attack speed items.
